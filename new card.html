Parrot

Parrots are intelligent birds. They have relatively large brains, they can learn, and they can use simple tools.
Because some species have the ability to make sounds like human voices and have plumages with bright colors

  • Seintific Name: Psittaciformes
  • Average Lengnth: 95 to 100 cm
  • Average Life: 80 years
  • Habitat: South America and Australasia

Parrots have a heavy, in relation to their size, and compact body with a large head and a short neck.
Their beaks are short, strong and curved. The two parts of the beak are very strong and used to break fruits and seeds. The tongue is large and strong.
